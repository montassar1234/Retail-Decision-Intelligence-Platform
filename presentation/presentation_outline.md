# Presentation Outline

## Slide 1: Title

Smart Retail Demand & Decision Intelligence  
AI + BI Academic Project

## Slide 2: Context and Problem

- Retail managers often rely on historical reports only.
- Traditional dashboards explain past results but do not predict future demand.
- Businesses need proactive, data-driven decision support.

## Slide 3: Proposed Solution

- BI dashboards for KPIs, trends, countries, and product categories
- AI forecasting model for the next 30 days of sales
- anomaly detection for abnormal spikes and drops
- product segmentation for strategic management
- recommendation engine for action points

## Slide 4: Dataset

- Open-source Online Retail dataset
- Transaction-level e-commerce records
- Includes invoice date, product, quantity, price, customer, and country

## Slide 5: Technical Workflow

1. Data cleaning and preprocessing
2. KPI and business analysis
3. Forecasting model
4. Anomaly detection
5. Product segmentation
6. Dashboard development

## Slide 6: BI Layer

- Total revenue
- Total profit
- Average order value
- Country and region performance
- Category profitability
- Time-series revenue trends

## Slide 7: AI Layer

- Demand forecasting
- Anomaly detection
- Product clustering
- Recommendation generation

## Slide 8: Expected Users

- Sales manager
- Operations manager
- Business analyst
- Supply planner

## Slide 9: Deliverables

- Clean dataset
- Forecast outputs
- Anomaly table
- Segmentation table
- Interactive dashboard
- Final report and GitHub repository

## Slide 10: Conclusion

- The project combines descriptive BI and predictive AI.
- It moves decision-making from reporting to action.
- It is realistic, explainable, and portfolio-ready.
